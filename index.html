Welcome to the 6Wave Experiences

The 6Wave Experience in Davos is an exclusive business and leisure destination program. We create upscale, tailored, private environments where leaders and impact investors can discuss major issues, discover the next big innovations and form crucial relationships that result in the substantial global changes needed to drive the next wave of the technological revolution.

6Wave is a think tank that studies the intersection of technological progress, social entertainment and the 4th Industrial Revolution. We structure problems and discuss solutions that have a major impact on global prosperity.

The company was founded by Evgeny Xata, the co-author of: Sixth Mega-Cycle: What can economic history teaches us about the future?

Important Notes:

We are committed to confidentiality of our VIP guests, when they prefer to remain “incognito”.

For the rest, the announcement of speakers and moderators will be published three days before the event, in order to accommodate VIPs.

* All our events have a time limit, as our space is super limited, relative to the amount of requests, please be mindful of the other participants. You can apply for the waiting list for the next event in case of a “no show”. Participants are randomly selected so everyone will have an equal chance.

** Due to the large amount of applications, we have to limit 2 events per person. We are respectful and fair to all our guests.

*** To attend our events, you must demonstrate a proven track record in high impact initiatives.
