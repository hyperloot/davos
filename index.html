Welcome to the 6Wave Experiences
Monaco, Milan, Davos, Bad Ragaz, Zurich 2020 Edition.

The 6Wave Experiences* exclusive business and leisure destination program. We create upscale, tailored, private environments where leaders and impact people can discuss major issues, have major discoveries, where strong relationships are formed, that result in big global changes, needed to drive the next wave of technological revolution. Annual membership is required to attend our events. We want High Impact People. Membership requires Referral & Invitation.

*Annual membership fee $5000. Our next event will be Formula One Monaco.

6Wave is a think tank that studies the intersection of technological progress, social entertainment and the 4th Industrial Revolution. We structure problems, discussion solutions that have a major impact on global prosperity. 6Wave was created by Evgeny Xata, the co-author of: Sixth Mega-Cycle: What can economic history teaches us about the future?

Important Notes:

We are committed to confidentiality of our VIP guests, when they prefer to remain “incognito”. For the rest the final decision on speakers and moderators will be made three days before the event, in order to accommodate VIPs.

**All our events have a time limit, as our space is super limited, relative to the amount of requests, please be mindful of the other participants. You can apply for the waiting list for the next event, in case of a “no shows”, also randomly selected so everyone will have an equal % chance.

***Due to the large amount of applications, we have to limit 2 events per person. We are respectful and fair to all our Guests.
